About

Carnage Music Label is a dynamic force in the electronic music industry, championing cutting-edge artists and groundbreaking sounds. Our mission is to elevate the genre and bring fresh, innovative music to the forefront. We are passionate about our work and committed to supporting our artists' growth and success.

Join us on our journey as we continue to redefine the electronic music scene and bring exceptional music to fans across the United States and beyond.
